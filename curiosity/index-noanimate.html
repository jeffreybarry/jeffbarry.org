Your lovely curiosity

Brings the joy of discovery.

Your soft face lights with excitement

When you learn of newfound species.

 

Your pace may slow as years mount up,

Only to find quickened steps as

You're pacing about the cabin

Of a vessel expediting

You towards enchanting lands where

Bats, ravens, and raptors roam the sky.

 

I remain forever with you,

Nestled in the warmth of

Your lovely curiosity.
