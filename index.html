Fundamental to my own professional interests—as a librarian, as a software developer, as a writer, as a reader—is understanding how we tell and read stories (particularly non-fiction) in digital media. The future of my young daughter’s life is the lens through which I view the changing shape of creativity, learning, and leisure in the early twenty-first century. Working with Internet technologies since 1990, I am an Associate Professor and Associate University Librarian at Washington and Lee University. I blog at Endless Hybrids.
